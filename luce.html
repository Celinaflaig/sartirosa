Driven by Emotion

"Born from the spirit of Italian joy.

We believe speed can be soft.

And power can be poetic."

Sarti Rosa was born from a simple belief: driving should feel like a perfect summer evening in Italy — warm, luminous, and unforgettable.

We translate the essence of Italian lifestyle into motion. Design inspired by light, sound, and the Mediterranean spirit. Sustainable electric vehicles infused with the sensuality of an Aperitivo sunset.

Sarti doesn't build cars. We create experiences on wheels. Each vehicle — from the elegant Luce to the powerful Ombra and playful Mare — is crafted for design-driven aesthetes who understand that true luxury is a feeling.
